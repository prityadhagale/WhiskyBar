Scotch Malt Whisky is a preeminent provider of high-end, rare, and collectible bottles and casks of its namesake. We provide the necessary structure allowing owners benefit from natural investment growth and safe storage of their collections. Innovation meets tradition, as each collectible is paired with a Non- Fungible Token, guaranteeing ownership, traceability, and auditability. Non-Fungible Tokens are traded in the virtual world on the blockchain, a form of decentralised public digital ledger, without the need to move or disturb the physical product. Ownership transfer is fast and secure, and guarantees that if the collectible is ever redeemed, only the owner of the NFT would be able to do that.
